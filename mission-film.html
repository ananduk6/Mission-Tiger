Our Film

Mission Tiger is a film featuring he root causes of tiger poaching in India and different sets of people who are for and against this .Through the story revolves around tiger poaching, this film has tried to showcase the Ground realities of a life of poor villagers, the victims and the forest officials who are directly or indirectly affected by the poaching of tigers. The director has indirectly tried to explain that our greed and exorbitance are the main cause of this biggest cat being endangered.

Ram Thappa though hails from a family of poachers leaves his only way of livelihood to find other means to look after his family. But circumtences pull him back to this felonious act . Jyostna Varma a journalist passionate about the wild especially on tigers is all set out to find the ground realities of poaching , she seeks the guidance of Mr.Vikram Nair for this venture.

Vijay Raaz is talented bollywood actor plays the leading role in this film as Ram Thappa. He has acted in many hindi film often handling comedy roles and mostly given the roles of common Indian man . His films include Reghu Romeo , Bhopal express and Monsoon wedding .
