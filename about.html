About

Mission Tiger is a film featuring he root causes of tiger poaching in India and different sets of people who are for and against this .Through the story revolves around tiger poaching, this film has tried to showcase the Ground realities of a life of poor villagers, the victims and the forest officials who are directly or indirectly affected by the poaching of tigers. The director has indirectly tried to explain that our greed and exorbitance are the main cause of this biggest cat being endangered.
